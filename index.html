Your Meal Just Made Charleston Better

When you dine at our partner restaurants, a portion of profits supports dozens of incredible local nonprofits changing lives in our community. Great food meets great causes in a model that turns business success into community impact.

Meet Some of the Nonprofits You're Supporting

AMOR Healing Kitchen
Fighting illness with food

AMOR delivers free, plant-based healing meals prepared by teen volunteers to people battling cancer and chronic illness. Your dinner helps provide a week of nourishing meals, snacks, and fresh flowers to someone who needs hope most.

100,000+ meals delivered

Doors to Dream
Building tomorrow's leaders

Doors to Dream mentors Charleston middle and high school students through service projects that transform both the students and their community. Your meal helps fund leadership programs creating the next generation of changemakers.

70 students mentored

Let's Walk
Keeping seniors moving

Let's Walk pairs trained volunteers with people who need companionship to get outside and stay active. Your dining helps fund this free service that's proven to dramatically improve health and happiness.

4,000+ walks completed

Charleston Hope
Mental health where kids need it

Charleston Hope provides mental health counseling directly in Title One schools during the school day, creating safe spaces where students can develop emotional resilience and build confidence.

500+ counseling hours annually

Shared City
Making charity more effective

Shared City trains nonprofit leaders, donors, and volunteers to create lasting change rather than temporary relief. Your meal helps strengthen the entire Charleston nonprofit community by making charitable work more impactful.

Dozens of nonprofits trained

Empowered Minds
Teaching mindfulness in schools

Empowered Minds cultivates learning-ready and compassionate classrooms by teaching social and emotional skills through yoga and mindfulness in Charleston County schools. Your meal gives students tools to control their emotions and build confidence.

1,000 students empowered weekly

Be A Mentor
One caring adult changes everything

Be A Mentor connects youth with caring adults who empower them to reach their full potential through one-on-one mentorship. Operating in 29 Lowcountry schools, they prove that every child is one caring relationship away from achieving their dreams.

400+ youth mentored

MAD PARK
Making health a way of life

MAD PARK empowers youth, families, and educators with access to wellness resources, creating a sustainable health-centered culture in Charleston. Your meal helps ensure that health isn't a privilege but a right for every generation.

Building healthier futures

How It Works

Plate to Purpose is a Charleston model that invests in passionate restaurateurs. When their businesses thrive, a portion of profits flows back to support local nonprofits through the Ripple Fund.

1

Investment

We provide startup capital and mentorship from someone who knows the restaurant business inside and out.

2

Growth

Restaurateurs build thriving businesses with ongoing support and connections to Charleston's hospitality community.

3

Impact

A portion of profits returns to fund local nonprofits, creating a ripple effect of positive change across Charleston.
